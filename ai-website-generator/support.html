Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first website?

To create your first website, sign up for an account, choose a template or start from scratch, and use our AI chat interface to describe what you want. The AI will generate a website based on your description, which you can then refine using our visual editor.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes, Pro and Business plan subscribers can use their own domain names. After publishing your website, go to the Settings page and follow the instructions to connect your custom domain.

How do I edit my website after it's published?

You can edit your website at any time by logging into your account and opening the project. Make your changes using the AI chat or visual editor, then click "Publish" to update your live website.

Can I export my website's code?

Yes, Business plan subscribers can export their website's HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code for use outside of our platform.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, go to the Account Settings page and click on "Subscription". From there, you can manage or cancel your subscription. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
