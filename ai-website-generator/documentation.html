Introduction

Welcome to the AI Website Generator documentation. This guide will help you understand how to use our platform to create beautiful, functional websites using our AI-powered tools.

AI Website Generator combines the power of artificial intelligence with intuitive design tools to make website creation accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill level.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Sign Up for an Account

    Create a free account to get started with AI Website Generator.

  2. Choose a Template

    Select from our library of templates or start from scratch.

  3. Chat with the AI

    Describe what you want your website to look like, and our AI will generate it for you.

  4. Refine and Edit

    Use the visual editor to make adjustments or continue chatting with the AI to refine your site.

  5. Publish Your Website

    When you're satisfied with your website, publish it with a single click.

Account Setup

Setting up your account is simple:

  1. Go to the Sign Up page
  2. Enter your name, email, and create a password
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile information (optional)

Once your account is set up, you'll have access to all the features included in your plan.
